# MSX GAMEPAD

## CONNECTOR
(Pad side.D-Sub9)

```
+-------------+
 \ 1 2 3 4 5 /
  \ 6 7 8 9 /
   +-------+
```

## PIN ASIGN

|No|I/O| Detail   |MD Pad|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| 1| - | UP   |1|
| 2| - | DOWN |2|
| 3| - | LEFT |3|
| 4| - | RIGHT|4|
| 5| I | VCC(5V)|5|
| 6| - | A button|9|
| 7| - | B button|6|
| 8| - | COM(GND)|(Don't connect 7 because it's selector.)|
| 9| - | GND     |8|

 I = Input to PAD
 
 O = Output from PAD

(Buttons are short circuit simply)
